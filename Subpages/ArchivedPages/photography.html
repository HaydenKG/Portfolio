Photography is a hobby that I picked up after graduating from high school in order to learn something new and be able to take amazing images on my journey through New Zealand back in 2018. I like the possibilities and freedom one can have with taking and manipulating the image.
3 rules I like to apply are the following

Rule of thirds 

                Dividing the frame into three equal parts.
                Aligning items within that frame or where the lines meet
                can help to align the composition.
                Hover over the image to see the rule applied >

Symmetry and patterns 

                Using existing symmetry and patterns can help
                to guide the viewers attention and align composition.
                Hover over the image to see the rule applied >

Framing 

                Using objects in the foreground to frame the subject.
                A frame can help to separate the image therefore adding depth. 
                It can also hide unwanted objects and guide the eye.
                Hover over the image to see the rule applied >

There are many more rules.
Naturally sometimes a number of them are applied at the same time.
Though occasionally the best rule is to break the rules and do something different.
